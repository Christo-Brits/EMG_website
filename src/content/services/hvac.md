## Complete HVAC Solutions for Commercial Buildings

Ethyl Merc Group provides end-to-end HVAC services that ensure optimal indoor climate, air quality, and energy efficiency for your commercial property. Our refrigeration and air conditioning technicians are fully licensed and experienced across all major brands and system types.

### Comfort, Compliance, and Cost Savings

Indoor air quality and temperature control directly impact staff productivity, tenant satisfaction, and regulatory compliance. Our HVAC team designs, installs, and maintains climate systems that balance performance with energy efficiency.

### Our HVAC Capabilities

- **Air Conditioning**: VRV/VRF systems, split systems, ducted solutions, and chiller plants
- **Ventilation**: Mechanical ventilation, heat recovery systems, and fresh air management
- **Heat Pumps**: Commercial-grade heat pump installation, repair, and optimisation
- **BMS Integration**: Smart building controls for automated climate and energy management
- **Air Quality**: CO2 monitoring, filtration upgrades, and Healthy Homes compliance

### Proactive Maintenance Programs

HVAC systems degrade 5% in efficiency annually without proper maintenance. Our preventative programs include seasonal filter changes, refrigerant checks, and performance testing that extend system life and reduce energy costs by up to 30%.

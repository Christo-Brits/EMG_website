## Professional Commercial Painting & Coatings

First impressions matter. Ethyl Merc Group's painting division delivers premium finishes and industrial-grade protective coatings that enhance the appearance and longevity of your commercial property. From single-level offices to multi-storey towers, we have the expertise and equipment to handle any project.

### More Than Just a Fresh Coat

Commercial painting requires specialist knowledge of surface preparation, coating systems, and environmental conditions. Our painters are trained in the latest application techniques and use premium products from leading manufacturers to ensure lasting results.

### Our Painting Capabilities

- **Interior Finishes**: Office spaces, retail environments, common areas, and tenant spaces
- **Exterior Coatings**: Weatherproof systems designed for New Zealand's demanding climate
- **Industrial Coatings**: Epoxy floors, anti-corrosion systems, and chemical-resistant finishes
- **Specialist Coatings**: Anti-graffiti treatments, fire-retardant coatings, and high-temperature paints
- **Roof Coatings**: Reflective and waterproof membrane systems

### Minimising Disruption

We understand that painting in operational buildings requires careful planning. Our teams work outside business hours, use low-VOC products, and maintain strict containment protocols to ensure minimal disruption to your tenants and operations.
